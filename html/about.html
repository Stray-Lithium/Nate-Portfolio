About me

I'm Nate, I have recently made a move to Iceland and have been settled into a software role for some time now. I love what I do and am still always looking into new things I can learn and new projects I can get involved in. I feel like my journey is still just beginning and I want to see how far I can take this.

It all started for me when I graduated from Northcoders which is a fullstack software development bootcamp. The 10 years previously for me employment wise have been customer facing roles; all the way from weddings to insurance sales and I always struggled with the concept of feeling like I've accomplished everything within any given role, when I know the job inside out, what is left? This is what prompted the change of career for me.

I had a friend recommend that I try programming as its not something I will ever be able to 'complete' and will always bring me a challenge, boy was he right. Since that conversation I have had the coding bug and I haven't been able stop, it excites me and it is amazing how there is just no limit to what can be achieved, code is all around us and I want to be a part of its future.

Another thing that is a big part of my life is anything creative, I have spent countless nights at my computer learning how to do something new whether it be learning an instrument, new software or how to make jewellery. I like to think this will benefit my coding progression from a visual perspective, I have had a lot of exposure to Photoshop along with other Adobe software's which means not only can I put all of these once thought useless skills to use but it also shows I am able to adapt to new things and want to learn them not only because I have to, but because I genuinely enjoy it.
